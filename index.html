How to use dofmojis as Stickers in different Apps?

Discord

Join AKL-Discord

WhatsApp

1. Download and unzip dofmojis.zip

2. Open WhatsApp Web

3. Go into any chat and click on the emoji

4. Create a new sticker

5. Select one of the dofmojis

6. Adjust to your liking and click on send

7. Done. Sticker can take a while to load, maybe refresh.

Repeat. Otherwise try different WhatsApp Sticker Apps, maybe they will let you import all at once.
